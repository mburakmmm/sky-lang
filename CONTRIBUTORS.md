## Get Involved

Ready to contribute? Here's how to get started:

1. **Read the Documentation**
   - [Contributing Guide](CONTRIBUTING.md)
   - [Grammar Specification](sky/docs/spec/grammar.md)
   - [API Documentation](https://docs.rs/sky)

2. **Join the Community**
   - [GitHub Discussions](https://github.com/sky-lang/sky/discussions)
   - [Discord Server](https://discord.gg/sky-lang)
   - [Twitter](https://twitter.com/SkyLanguage)

3. **Start Contributing**
   - Pick an issue labeled "good first issue"
   - Fork the repository
   - Make your changes
   - Submit a pull request

4. **Get Recognized**
   - Your contributions will be listed here
   - You'll receive recognition in release notes
   - You'll be invited to the contributors team
